Why Enroll in Joyland School?

Enrolling in PHINMA UPang gives you the privilege of having excellent education in a vibrant community that is nearer to home.

What are the courses?

PHINMA UPang offers more than 20 undergraduate courses and programs taught in innovative methods to prepare graduates to excel in the competitive professional world.

How can I enroll?

OFFICIAL ENROLLMENT STEPS

    1.1 Fill up Student Permanent Record (SPR) & Info Sheet

    1.2 Verification of Required Documents (if available):

  • Form 138
  • NSO Birth Certificate

    • Downpayment

  • If enrollee has Slot Reservation: Non-scholars: pay an additional P2,000.00 downpayment to get ID & 1 set of uniform For scholars: can pay P750.00 for ID or P1,500 for both ID & 1 set of uniform
  • If enrollee has NO Slot Reservation: Non-scholars: pay P2,500.00 downpayment to get ID & 1 set of uniform For HK scholars: can pay P750.00 for ID or P1,500 for both ID & 1 set of uniform

    • Printing of Official Registration Form (ORF) or Temporary Registration Form (TRF) in case schedule is not yet ready.

SLOT RESERVATION

Through Slot Reservation, an enrollee is ensured of enlistment in the succeeding school year, saving time and effort in the enrollment process.

    1.1 Fill up Student Permanent Record (SPR) & Info Sheet

    1.2 Verification of Required Documents (if available):

  • Form 138
  • NSO Birth Certificate

    • 1.3 Downpayment

  • (Required) Applicant pays for PhP 500 minimum down payment.

    • 1.4 Issuance of Official Receipt (OR) of down payment.

    1.5 Issuance of Reservation/ Welcome Letter.
