Admission

    Eligibility: All students must meet the age and grade level requirements for the school.

    Application: Interested Applicants must complete and submit a school admission application form, including all required information and documents..

    Testing: All applicants may be required to take an admission test to assess their academic abilities and aptitude. The test may include a written examination, an interview, or other assessments.

    Interviews: Selected applicants may be invited for an interview with the school administration to assess their motivation, interest, and fit with the school's values and culture.

    Supporting Documents: Applicants must submit the following supporting documents along with their application form:

  • Copies of the student's most recent report card and transcript
  • A copy of the student's birth certificate or passport
  • Any other required documents specified by the school

    Notification: The school will notify applicants of their admission decision within [insert time frame].
