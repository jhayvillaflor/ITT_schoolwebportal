Alumni Homecoming 2023

A new technology school has opened its doors in the heart of downtown, with a mission to prepare students for the jobs of the future. The school, called FutureTech Academy, is a state-of-the-art facility designed to teach students the latest in emerging technologies and equip them with the skills they need to thrive in the 21st century workforce.

FutureTech Academy is the brainchild of a group of tech industry veterans who saw a gap in the market for a school that could give students hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology. The school boasts a wide range of courses in fields such as artificial intelligence, robotics, data science, and cybersecurity, among others.
