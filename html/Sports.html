Athletics

Joyland School of Technology Athletic Program focuses to collaborate with parents and staff to ensure our students continue to have a sports experience that can be rewarding for everyone. The coaches and staff are readily awaiting to provide our students with the best sports experience to help develop their skills, academic success, and continued athletic development.

Teams

The program offers 4 Junior Varsity/Varsity teams over the course of 2 seasons which includes Volleyball, Girls Cross Country, Boys Cross Country, Girls Basketball, Boys Basketball, and Softball, Girls Track and Field, and Boys Track and Field.
