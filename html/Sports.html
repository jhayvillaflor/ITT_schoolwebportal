Athletics

Joyland School of Technology Athletic Program focuses to collaborate with parents and staff to ensure our students continue to have a sports experience that can be rewarding for everyone. The coaches and staff are readily awaiting to provide our students with the best sports experience to help develop their skills, academic success, and continued athletic development.

Teams

The program offers 4 Junior Varsity/Varsity teams over the course of 2 seasons which includes Volleyball, Girls Cross Country, Boys Cross Country, Girls Basketball, Boys Basketball, and Softball, Girls Track and Field, and Boys Track and Field.

Game day Details

We are proud to invite families, students, faculty, staff, recruiters, and fans alike to 'The Chamber', Sci High's Gymnatorium for athletic events. In the sections below are details of what to expect for gameday's at Sci High.

What time do the doors open for gameday events at Sci High?

Doors typically open 30 minutes before the start time of the event.

Is there a cost for attending Sci High gameday events?

Yes, there is a small admission fee for attending Sci High gameday events. Prices vary depending on the event and can be found on the school's website.

Can I bring outside food and drinks to Sci High gameday events?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside 'The Chamber'. However, there is a concession stand available with a variety of snacks and drinks for purchase.

Are there designated seating areas for students and families?

Yes, there are designated seating areas for students and families. Students typically sit in the student section, while families can sit in the general seating area.

Is parking available on-site for Sci High gameday events?

Parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis in the school's parking lot. It is recommended to arrive early to ensure a parking spot. Additionally, street parking may be available in the surrounding neighborhood.
